# UIDT Scope and Non-Scope

**Stratum: I–III (see column)**  
**Maintainer: P. Rietz**  
**Last reviewed: 2026-05-10**

This document defines what UIDT v3.9 explains, what it addresses partially, and what it
explicitly does **not** (yet) explain.  Transparency about absences is a core epistemic
commitment of the framework.

## Limitation L9 (New)

> **L9**: UIDT mathematics operates in the continuum (ℝ⁴, differential equations, path
> integrals).  The ontological thesis that information distinctions are discrete has not
> been formally reconciled with this continuum mathematics.  The emergent-continuum
> derivation is an open research objective classified as Evidence E.
