# UIDT Known Limitations — Central Registry

**Authoritative list for UIDT v3.9. All other documents MUST reference by L-ID.**  
New limitations must be added here first. Resolved entries are marked `[RESOLVED vX.Y]` but never deleted.

## L1 — Electron Mass Residual
Not derived from first principles within UIDT v3.9. Residual between UIDT prediction and measured value reflects unresolved leptonic coupling.  
**Status:** Open | **Evidence:** D

## L2 — Phenomenological γ Parameter
γ = 16.339 is calibrated to the hadronic spectrum, not derived from a more fundamental principle.  
**Status:** Open | **Evidence:** A− (phenomenological)

## L3 — Cosmological Calibration
w₀, Ω_Λ, H₀ are calibrated against DESI-Y1 / Planck 2018. H₀ tension and S₈ tension are **not declared resolved** by UIDT.  
**Status:** Open | **Evidence:** C

## L4 — Single-Author Verification
All numerical verifications performed within the UIDT Python codebase by the framework author. No independent external reproduction completed.  
**Status:** Open | See INDEPENDENT_REPRODUCTION_PROTOCOL.md

## L5 — LLM Audit Scope
LLM audits are auxiliary tools only (format, dimensions, algebra). They do not constitute independent peer review. Shared architecture and training-data correlations are systematic limitations no prompt engineering can overcome.  
**Status:** Structural (managed, not resolvable)

## L6 — Clay Submission Status
UIDT Banach proof prepared but not reviewed by Clay Mathematics Institute or any independent mathematician. Status: *submitted candidate*, not *accepted proof*.  
**Status:** Open | **Evidence:** B

## L7 — No Direct Measurement of S(x)
S(x) has no direct experimental measurement protocol. All experimental connections are indirect proxies. A Born-rule equivalent for S(x) does not yet exist.  
**Status:** Open | **Evidence:** D

## L8 — Single Renormalization Scheme (MS̄)
All numerical results derived within MS̄ only. Scheme-independence of Δ*, γ, RG fixed point not verified across MOM or Wilsonian schemes.  
**Status:** Open | **Evidence:** C

## L9 — Missing Derivations (BH Entropy, Gravity, SU(3) Origin)
UIDT v3.9 does not derive: Bekenstein–Hawking entropy S=A/4, graviton as dynamical spin-2 field, or origin of SU(3) from information geometry.  
**Status:** Open | **Evidence:** E

## L10 — Continuum vs. Discrete Ontology Tension
UIDT uses continuous mathematics while the foundational ontology posits discrete information distinctions. Formal derivation of the continuum limit from discrete UIDT micro-dynamics does not exist.  
**Status:** Open | **Evidence:** E

## L11 — Mathematical Isomorphism Hypothesis
UIDT ontology implicitly assumes the mathematical structure is isomorphic to physical reality, not merely a good effective description. This strong claim is Evidence E and untested empirically.  
**Status:** Open | **Evidence:** E

## L12 — Experimental Feasibility of Kill-Switch Tests
Feasibility of kill-switch experiments (Casimir at 0.66 nm, photonic n_crit, precision E_T) not independently assessed by experimental physicists.  
**Status:** Open | **Evidence:** D  
**Recommended action:** Consult at least one experimental physicist per kill-switch domain.
