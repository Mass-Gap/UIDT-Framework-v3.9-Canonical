§4 · Known Limitations — Transparency Protocol
LIMITATION POLICY — ACTIVE

Per UIDT Constitution, the following limitations must be acknowledged in any publication or PR referencing this roadmap:

1. κ-parameter phenomenology: γ = 16.339 [A−] is phenomenological — no first-principles derivation from QCD.
2. Electron mass residual: Known discrepancy in electron mass prediction remains unresolved — do not claim resolution.
3. NCF application to UIDT: NCF proofs (Park et al.) apply to Wasserstein-2 OT with quadratic cost. Applicability to Yang–Mills vacuum manifold is a conjecture [Evidence D] — not demonstrated.
4. H₀ and S₈ tensions: UIDT cosmological parameters are consistent with some observations but the cosmological tensions are NOT resolved — per UIDT language rules: "UIDT suggests compatibility with DESI-DR2 data" only.
5. HJ-Reachability paper [AUDIT_PENDING]: Paper 3 is under review with no confirmed arXiv/DOI — all claims derived from it remain at Evidence D or lower until publication.
